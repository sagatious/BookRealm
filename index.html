James Clear
(4/5)

Atomic Habits offers a proven framework for improving--every day. James Clear, one of the world's leading experts on habit formation, reveals practical strategies that will teach you exactly how to form good habits, break bad ones, and master the tiny behaviors that lead to remarkable results.

Carol S. Dweck
(5/5)

After decades of research, world-renowned Stanford University psychologist Carol S. Dweck, Ph.D., discovered a simple but groundbreaking idea: the power of mindset. In this brilliant book, she shows how success in school, work, sports, the arts, and almost every area of human endeavor can be dramatically influenced by how we think about our talents and abilities.

Toni Morrison
(4/5)

Staring unflinchingly into the abyss of slavery, this spellbinding novel transforms history into a story as powerful as Exodus and as intimate as a lullaby. Sethe, its protagonist, was born a slave and escaped to Ohio, but eighteen years later she is still not free. She has too many memories of Sweet Home, the beautiful farm where so many hideous things happened.

Cal Newport
(4.5/5)

Deep work is the ability to focus without distraction on a cognitively demanding task. It's a skill that allows you to quickly master complicated information and produce better results in less time. In this book, author and professor Cal Newport flips the narrative on impact in a connected age.

Yuval Noah Harari
(4.5/5)

Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind is a book by Yuval Noah Harari that offers a wide-ranging history of the human species. It examines the cognitive revolution, agricultural revolution, and unification of humankind, among other topics, to provide a big-picture look at human development.

Héctor García and Francesc Miralles
(4.5/5)

Ikigai: The Japanese Secret to a Long and Happy Life explores the concept of ikigai, which roughly translates to "reason for being" or "purpose in life." Authors Héctor García and Francesc Miralles delve into the habits and practices of the residents of Okinawa, Japan, who are known for their longevity and well-being, to uncover the secrets to living a fulfilling life.
